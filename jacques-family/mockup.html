Process

Our construction process has been created to ensure that we meet and exceed all of our customers' needs. We achieve this by diligently taking the steps necessary to allow for effective project supervision and to convey to our customers that their satisfaction and well-being are our top priorities

Phase One Discovery

We begin every client relationship with a brief 20-minute call. Then, the true discovery phase takes place starting with a face-to-face meeting and following with an on-site visit. By the end of this phase, we will have a mutual understanding your vision and the steps to get there.

Phase Two Planning

Our strategic approach to budgeting ensures you dictate the project's direction before construction begins. We initiate with a preliminary design and a budget forecast, followed by working collaboratively with you to make informed decisions, fine-tuning your concept to align with your financial expectations.

Phase Three Execution

Watch your unique vision materialize before your eyes, from the moment we start the build to the day you move in. Experience the spirit of respect, collabiration, and dedication every step of the journey.
